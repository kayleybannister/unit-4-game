Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed. If your score goes above the random number, you lose the game.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal.

Every time the game starts, the random number and the value of each crystal will change.

Good luck!
